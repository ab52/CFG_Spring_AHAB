What we do

A gastronomic experience

Is there a better pair than wine and cheese? We agree - there is not but when it comes to choosing which tipple to match a cheese with, the decision can be daunting.

Our workshops offer a simple guide to create the perfect pairing.

We offer two packages:

  • The Brie-lliant package: 9 intensive sessions, covering basics on wine, cheese and pairing
  • The meal deal: a 4 hour cheese and wine feast offering introductions to pairing cheese and wine, but first and foremost, an excellent experience to enjoy with family, friends or colleagues.

Check our Contact page to book your spot in the Brie-lliant sessions.

Alternatively, give us a call to book your meal deal.
